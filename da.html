LiveStatus APP Account deletion

How do I delete my account and personal data?

Method I:

Please log in to your LiveStatus APP — Click on the profile page in the upper left cornerClick on the 'Settings' icon in the upper right corner— — and then enter "Account and Security" — Select 'Delete my account'—Confirmation of deletion

Method II:

  1. Please use the email address of your registered account to contact our review team( livehousetech@gmail.com) with the username and user ID of the account you want to delete.
  2. When we receive your deletion request, we will review the info you provide first and email back a deletion confirmation notice shortly given the correct info. Please note that it's better to feedback the notice within 7 days otherwise the request could be invalid again.
  3. Once we receive your reconfirmation email, we will delete your account and personal data within 30 days. Besides, we will notify you by email after the deletion is completed.

Note 1: Deleting your account will simultaneously delete your user information and data. Once deleted, it can not be recovered, so please operate with caution.

Note 2: This deletion can take up to 30 days. If you log in again within 30 days after deleting your account, it means that the deleted account will still be invalid.

Will deleting my "account and data" directly pause my Super subscription as well?

No - if you subscribed to Super Answer.Al via the app before, deleting your account will not suspend charges since subscription is managed by Apple or Google. You must cancel your subscription renewal before proceeding with data deletion.
